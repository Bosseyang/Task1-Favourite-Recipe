My Favourite Recipe

This recipe is my own take of the famous Italian Bolognese. The reason to why this is my favourite recipe is bacause it is a very filling recipe with alot of depth in taste and works equally well as a leftover dish as when eaten fresh. I usually do not limit my use of Bolognese with just pasta I make Bolognese sandwiches, pizza etc with it aswell. It also servs perfectly as a meat sauce base for lasagna!
